Hi there! My name is Kevin Zhao and I'm a digital graphic designer. I worked at a design agency for 1.5 years and have been freelance for six. Currently I work as an Academic Technology administrator, providing workshop and training for all kinds of technology.

I have four years of experience as Information Technology Support for New York University, at the Steinhardt School of Culture, Education, and Human Development. I'm based out of New York City and enjoy playing tennis and strategy games. I'm also constantly looking for new music and the next earworm song.
